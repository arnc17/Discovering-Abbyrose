    My Family Background

    My family is my greatest treasure, and alongside that, my cultural heritage holds immense significance. My dad's background is a captivating blend of Irish and Vietnamese roots; he was born in Vietnam and spent his early years in Saudi Arabia before coming to California for schooling. My mom's heritage intertwines Irish and Mexican influences. I have fond memories of speaking Spanish with my family and our cherished annual tradition of making tamales at Christmas. Homemade Vietnamese dishes remain close to my heart. Although I'm not fluent in the language, I embrace the customs that grace my life and celebrations. As someone with a multicultural background, I sometimes grapple with questions of identity, but I'm steadfast in my commitment to embracing every facet of my diverse heritage.
